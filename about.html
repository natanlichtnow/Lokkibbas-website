About Us

At Moores Waterproofing we endeavour to do our best work every day. As part of our commitment to leading the industry in quality, we keep up to date on decking, roofing and waterproofing methods as well as changing products. Our roofing team is certified by Certainteed as their Master Shingle Applicators & Soprema Roofing Products. The extensive training our team completes allows Moores Waterproofing to offer Platinum and 5 Star warranties on all of our Weatherproofing products in Fort St. John. We have also become Certainteed Shingle Technology Specialists and promise to continue achieving industry standards, building code and protocol!

Built on over 12 years of experience, Moores Waterproofing is serving Fort St. John and the Peace Region. We offer a wide variety of roofing, vinyl decking, siding installations. While staying 110% dedicated to the satisfaction of our clients, Moores Waterproofing completes our projects in a professional and timely manner in Fort St. John. Have a Moores Waterproofing team member come by and break down our scope of work, warranty information and help you make an informed decision on quality vinyl decking, roofing and waterproofing products as well as ventilation solutions for your home!
