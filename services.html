Our Services

Roofing

High quality roofing installations and repairs.

Moores Waterproofing will help you choose the best option based on your needs for roof longevity and aesthetic preferences. We use building envelope technology to ensure complete waterproofing below the shingle layer, around chimneys, skylights and other roof protrusions. Moores Waterproofing is your trusted roofing contractor in Fort St. John.

Vinyl Decking

Duradek vinyl decking solutions for lasting performance.

Moores Waterproofing is your vinyl deck expert in Fort St. John. 100% maintenance-free and waterproof, our vinyl membranes come with a written warranty along with a Moores Waterproofing 5 year workmanship guarantee. We have many different colours and thicknesses to meet your design and budget needs.

Siding

Professional siding contractor services.

Moores Waterproofing is a Fort St. John siding contractor, offering installations on vinyl siding as well as fibre cement siding products. Hardie siding is a durable, weather resistant fibre cement siding. There are many styles including shingle, shake and Hardie panel siding. These products carry a 30 year material warranty.
